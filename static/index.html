Prompt Injection

Attackers hide instructions to trick your AI into breaking its rules. We detect jailbreak attempts, encoded payloads, and manipulation before they reach the model.

Data Leakage

Your emails, phone numbers, passwords, and financial data stay yours. Every prompt and response is scanned so private information never leaks to unauthorized parties.

Malicious Code

AI outputs can contain dangerous code that deletes files or exfiltrates data. We identify and block harmful commands before they execute on your system.

Data Flow Tracking

Ever wonder where your data ends up? We map every destination, inspect headers and payloads, and flag suspicious endpoints so you always remain in control.

Rogue Agents

Autonomous agents can act without oversight. We detect hidden workflows, automation chains, and unauthorized actions in real time to prevent runaway behavior.

Policy Enforcement

Define exactly what your AI may and may not do. If an action falls outside your rules, we block it instantly and alert you. No exceptions, no surprises.
